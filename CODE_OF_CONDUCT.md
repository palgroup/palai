# Palai Code of Conduct

Palai contributors must keep project spaces welcoming, professional, and safe.

## Expected behavior

- Discuss ideas and code on their technical merits.
- Give specific, constructive feedback and accept corrections in good faith.
- Respect different backgrounds, experience levels, identities, and viewpoints.
- Protect private information, credentials, vulnerability details, and user data.
- Stop behavior that a maintainer identifies as disruptive or unsafe.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized attention, deliberate intimidation,
doxxing, credential disclosure, and sustained disruption are not permitted in any
project space or when representing Palai.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers. Security
vulnerabilities follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md), not the conduct-report path.
Maintainers may remove content, restrict participation, or ban contributors when
needed to protect the community. Reports are handled as confidentially as the
investigation permits, and retaliation against reporters is prohibited.
